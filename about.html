Our Mission

Assistant systems over random agents.

We build assistants that are production-ready from day one: telemetry-first, auditable, and outcome-driven. No AI slop, no disconnected bots—just a cohesive assistant system that your team can trust in production.

Our Story

We started XenTeck while working full-time and paying bills, armed with deep experience in AI tools, coding, media, and operations. After building and shipping for clients across stacks, we became convinced that assistant systems—not one-off agents—are how companies will actually work.
