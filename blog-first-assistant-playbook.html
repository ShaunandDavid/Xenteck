A Simple Playbook for Your First Assistant

Start with one outcome, wire telemetry, and make the assistant prove value before you scale.

Pick one measurable outcome

Choose the workflow where speed or quality matters most: inbound response, pipeline hygiene, or support triage.

Wire telemetry first

Instrument inputs, decisions, and outputs so you know when the assistant helped, hurt, or stalled.

Ship, review, iterate

Launch with approvals, then relax guardrails as the assistant earns trust. Rollbacks stay one click away.
